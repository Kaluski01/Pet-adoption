Whether you want to adopt a playmate or a snuggle buddy, the cats and kittens for adoption from CAT are ready to join your family. Let the matchmaking begin!

Make a Friend

To see cats and kittens ready for adoption now, please scroll down and wait a moment for the page to load.

  • To learn more about each kitty, click on their photo below to view their pet profile.
  • Please read each pet’s profile carefully to see where they are located and follow the instructions to take the next steps toward adoption.
  • The adoption process—including meet-and-greet options—varies based on the kitty’s location.
  • Be sure to review our adoption policies and adoption process details.
  • All cats and kittens adopted from CAT will be spayed or neutered, current on vaccinations, and microchipped before going home. Please see our adoption fees for more details about everything included with your adoption.

To see more photos and info about some kittens and cats in foster care, follow CAT foster families on social media.

We look forward to connecting you with a new furry friend!
